BPO

NAPTEL offers domain specific BPO services targeted at verticals such as finance, manufacturing, retail and distribution. These services help customers in improving their business performance with great efficiency and saves operational costs incurred on their IT and human resources.

We provide timely and high value support services to our clients. We have developed a dedicated approach to BPO services which starts with critical analysis of the business processes involved at clientâ€™s end. We usually propose a plan to the client to manage their business processes by leveraging more cost effective resources working from our premises. Our approach starts with understanding and measuring the current processes followed, establishing quality controls, benchmarking against best practices, implementing performance improvement measures and then validating the performance of the total system regularly.

BPO services are designed and executed with proven technology based platforms and software applications. We recruit talented professionals to meet business requirements of our clients. These services enable our clients to focus on their businesses, while operational supports and needs are taken care of by our BPO service personnel.
